Meet The Modern Logging Solution

Built to decrease time between data input and log output, increase your control over output templates, and foster collaboration on a single platform

Your logs, anywhere

Access Liftoff using whichever device is available at the moment.

Bring Liftoff with you, whether you are working from your desktop in the office, your laptop in a client meeting, or on your phone from site. Liftoff dynamically scales the user interface to allow you to be productive from any screen size.

Search it!

Find what you're looking for.

Always have the information you need using the smart search function to find past projects and logs using project names, borehole numbers, and tags. Filter your search results by date and sharing options.

Two is better than one

Get another pair of eyes for double the checking.

Collaboration is built into Liftoff, be invited or invite others to review or edit your borehole logs without the need to send a file.

  • Set reminders for logs requested for review
  • Filter your review requests by due date
  • Choose the type of access other get when you share your logs
  • Set due dates for review requests and alert peers if a review is past due

It's all there

Enter everything on one page.

No more running through the tabs to find the right section for each data type, all borehole information is added on a single input page.

  • Drag and drop the data to be entered
  • Intuitive UI design to minimize time spent entering data
  • Find connections between data and catch mistakes before the logs are created.
  • Review your data to get the bigger picture or inspect individual testing results at the click of a button
